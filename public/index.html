About Me!

I am a junior majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics at Trinity University in San Antonio, Texas. When I am not at school, I live back home in Cypress, Texas, just outside of Houston.

I started my coding journey in 2015 when I registered for Computer Programming 1 during my sophomore year at Cypress Woods High School (212, CDUB) where I created Scratch games and started learning Python. After that, it has been a non-stop road of learning and delight. I have picked up many languages, platforms, and technologies along the way making each step more exciting than the last. My favorite language to use is Dart, though it's not very useful outside of Flutter. I am ecstatic to say that I will be working as a Software Engineer Intern at Coinbase this 2021 summer.

In the marginal free time afforded to me, I enjoy everything basketball: watching the NBA, running pick-up games, playing NBA2K. I also watch some football when I can. I am a huge fan of the San Antonio Spurs and LA Chargers. To blow off steam, I play video games with friends or by myself.
